## 🧾 Why SOPs Matter

Dynamic Apron isn’t just about recipes or bundles — it’s about **systems that scale**.  
Founder Daniel brings years of experience across restaurants and hospital kitchens, where consistency, timing, and clarity are the difference between chaos and seamless service.

- **Kitchen Discipline → SOPs**  
  Every station in a kitchen runs on rhythm: prep, line, expo, dish. Standard Operating Procedures capture that rhythm so it can be repeated, trained, and scaled.

- **Hospital Precision → Operations**  
  In healthcare food service, dietary restrictions, sanitation, and coordination across dozens of staff demand airtight processes. That same operational clarity drives Dynamic Apron’s consulting binders.

- **Front‑of‑House Awareness → Service SOPs**  
  Guest experience doesn’t stop at the plate. Service SOPs ensure communication, timing, and hospitality are as consistent as the food.

- **Cross‑Training → Scalable Systems**  
  Having worked nearly every position in the kitchen, Daniel understands how each role contributes to the whole. Dynamic Apron’s SOPs unify Culinary, Service, and Operations into one modular framework.

## 🚀 Deployment

Dynamic Apron is deployed using modern hosting platforms that integrate directly with GitHub.

### Vercel (Primary Deployment)
- **Automatic builds**: Every push to the `main` branch triggers a new deployment.  
- **Dashboard**: View all builds and logs in your [Vercel dashboard](https://vercel.com/daniel-meadows-projects/~/deployments).  
- **Custom domains**: Configure `dynamicapron.com` or other domains in Vercel’s settings.  
- **Preview deployments**: Pull requests generate preview URLs for testing before merging.  
- **Environment variables**: Managed securely in the Vercel dashboard.
